About the Author:

Frances Ojeda-Diaz was born in Walla Walla, Washington. They graduated from Lincoln Alternative Highschool in 2020 and graduated from the Walla Walla Community college in 2022.

Right now they are living in Portland, Oregon where they are attending Epicodus while working at Sephora on the side. They love skincare and have started to have an interest in abstract makeup since starting their job. They really enjoy helping people everyday, so if you ever have any questions about your skin needs, hit them up!

Some of the things Frances enjoys are:

  • literature
  • cooking
  • dreaming
  • acting afool

They enjoy creating in the form of tattooing, writing, drawing, textiles and coding, and hope to find a way to bridge all those mediums together in the future.

Frances joined Epicodus to become a developer who can make websites as beautiful as their art. They'd like to work in a field that will allow them to create, problem solve, and help others.

They hope to work at a company that focuses on making websites and tools for creatives of all kinds to help share their art with the world.

In the mean time they will spend their time coding, and taking camping breaks whenever they can.
